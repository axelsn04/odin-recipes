Brownies Recipe

These brownies are rich, fudgy, and perfect for chocolate lovers!

Ingredients (Makes about 16 brownies)

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line an 8x8-inch (20x20 cm) baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Add the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder to the mixture. Stir until just combined. If using, fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  6. Let cool in the pan before cutting into squares.
  7. Enjoy your delicious brownies!

These brownies are perfect for a sweet treat or dessert. Serve them warm with a scoop of ice cream for an extra indulgence!

Note: You can add nuts, caramel, or other mix-ins to customize your brownies!
