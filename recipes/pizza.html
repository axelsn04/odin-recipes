Pizza Recipe

Who doesn't love a good pizza?

Ingredients:

    For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110°F)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    • For the tomato sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the toppings:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Step 1: The pizza dough

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise for 1 hour.

    5. Step 2: The sauce

  5. In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.

    7. Step 3: Assemble the pizza

  7. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  8. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out on a floured surface.
  9. Spread the tomato sauce over the rolled-out dough.
  10. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  11. Add pepperoni slices and sprinkle with parmesan cheese and oregano.

    12. Step 4: Bake

  12. Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  13. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.

    14. Step 5: Serve

  14. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  15. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
  16. Enjoy your homemade pizza!

This recipe is a classic favorite, perfect for family gatherings or a cozy night in. Feel free to customize with your favorite toppings!

Note: You can also use store-bought pizza dough and sauce for a quicker option!
