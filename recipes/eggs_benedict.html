Eggs Benedict Recipe

A delicious and elegant breakfast choice!

Ingredients:

    For the poached eggs:

  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • Water (for poaching)

    • For the hollandaise sauce:

  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For assembly:

  • 2 English muffins, split and toasted
  • 4 slices Canadian bacon or ham
  • Chopped fresh chives or parsley (for garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Make the hollandaise sauce.

  1. In a heatproof bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and lemon juice.
  2. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and whisk continuously until the mixture thickens.
  3. Slowly drizzle in the melted butter while whisking until fully combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Remove from the heat and keep warm.

Step 2: Poach the eggs.

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a gentle simmer. Add vinegar and salt.
  2. Crack each egg into a small bowl, then gently slide it into the simmering water.
  3. Poach for about 3-4 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
  4. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.

Step 3: Assemble the dish.

  1. On each toasted English muffin half, place a slice of Canadian bacon.
  2. Top with a poached egg and drizzle with hollandaise sauce.
  3. Garnish with chopped chives or parsley.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

This recipe is a classic brunch favorite, perfect for special occasions or a weekend treat. Enjoy your Eggs Benedict!

Feel free to customize with your favorite ingredients, such as spinach or smoked salmon!

Note: Poaching eggs can be tricky, but with practice, you'll master it!
